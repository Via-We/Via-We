About Us

Empowering Growth with Strategic Business Solutions

Via-We Services Private Limited is a trusted business support company dedicated to empowering businesses and individuals. Founded in 2017 as MEGHDOOTH, we rebranded in 2021 to honor the legacy of our late co-founder, Shiva Dattu. Since then, we’ve been on a mission to deliver comprehensive solutions in manpower services, business consultancy, and marketing.

Krishnaveni Budati serves as the Managing Director of VIA-WE Services, bringing a visionary approach and a steadfast commitment to excellence across all areas of the business. As the life partner and dedicated business collaborator of our founder,Vijay Budati,Krishnaveni plays a pivotal role in shaping the company’s strategic direction and operational growth.

At Via-We Services Private Limited, we specialize in delivering both skilled and unskilled workforce solutions tailored to the unique needs of diverse industries. Our trusted partnerships with top-tier MNCs likeKFC, Burger Kingand Pantaloons reflect our commitment to quality and reliability. Alongside our workforce solutions, we offer comprehensive digital and offline marketing services designed to drive brand growth through creative content and data-driven strategies. We’re also proud to be associated with esteemed companies such as Jain Silver Plaza, Bhanu Special Castings, Roheetha Printers, Samrajya Silver Kingdom, and Ghar Sansar Interior Design — each a shining example of legacy and excellence in their field.

Our commitment includes guaranteed investment protection—ensuring our partners and clients never face a loss. With daily updates, tailored service plans, and a strong offline marketing team, we are the go-to solution for sustainable business growth.

Join hands with Via-We and let us transform your vision into success. Your growth is our mission.
